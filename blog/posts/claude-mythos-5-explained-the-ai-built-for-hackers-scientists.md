Mythos 5 is built on top of the Claude Fable 5 engine. But there is a big difference: Anthropic has removed the usual "safety rails" for trusted partners. This means the AI can do things that were previously blocked, like helping experts find security holes or running complex science experiments.

*   **Top Scores:** It is breaking records on major cybersecurity tests like Firefox and OSS-Fuzz.

## How do you get it?

Because this model is so powerful, you can’t just sign up for it on a website. Anthropic is being very careful about who gets to use it. They are calling this restricted access **"Project Glasswing."**

They also have some strict rules for companies using it:

*   **Data Rules:** Any data you put into Mythos 5 is deleted after 30 days. This keeps things secure and private.
*   **Controlled Access:** Only specific partners who have been vetted by Anthropic are allowed to turn this model on.
