## Big News for OpenClaw Users!

Hi everyone! I’m Robby. As an engineer who builds AI systems every day, I know how frustrating it is when your tools crash or act glitchy. That’s why I am so excited to share the new OpenClaw 4.21 update with you. 

This update is all about making your AI agent workflow more stable and easier to manage. Here is what you need to know.

### No More Random Crashes

If you have dealt with annoying memory crashes in the past, today is your lucky day. We completely fixed the memory bugs that were causing those mid-project shutdowns. Now, your AI can run longer and handle bigger jobs without stopping.

### Watch Your Wallet

One of the coolest features in this update is the new **cost observability tool**. Now, you can see how much your API usage is costing you in real-time. It’s a great way to stay on budget while you build your projects.

### Better Performance for macOS

Do you use a Mac? We hear you! We fixed an issue that was causing dropped messages. We increased the timeout for sending text, which means your communication stays steady and strong. We also fixed those pesky plugin ID issues so everything should load perfectly.

### Tips for a Smooth Update

Before you jump in, here are a few things to keep in mind:

* **Check the Configs:** We added stability updates to your job configurations. Double-check your settings to make sure they match the new standards.
* **Watch for Breaking Changes:** This update has some important changes. Make sure you read the update notes carefully.
* **Need to Roll Back?** Don’t worry! If something doesn't work for your specific setup, we have included a clear guide on how to go back to the previous version.

These stability updates are a huge step forward for the OpenClaw community. I’ve been testing them in my own work, and it makes a world of difference. Happy coding!
