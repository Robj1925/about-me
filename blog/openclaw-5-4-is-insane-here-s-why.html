OpenClaw 5.4 Is Here: Meet Your New AI Meeting Assistant

OpenClaw 5.4 has arrived with exciting updates, including a powerful new Google Meet agent that helps you take notes and chat in real-time.

OpenClaw 5.4: A Huge Step Forward

Hi everyone! I’m Robby. As a software engineer, I am always looking for ways to make my daily work easier. The new OpenClaw 5.4 update just dropped, and it is a game-changer for anyone who uses AI agents to get things done.

Meet Your New AI Meeting Assistant

The biggest change in this version is the new Google Meet Agent Mode. Imagine having an AI buddy that can jump into your video calls with you. This agent can:

  • Take notes: It listens to the conversation and writes down the important stuff so you don't have to.
  • Talk back: It can interact with other people on the call in real-time.

It’s like having a helpful assistant sitting right next to you during every meeting.

Other Great Improvements

Beyond the cool meeting agent, the team polished a few things to make the software run smoother:

  • Better Logs: Have you ever had your logs cut off right when you needed them? Those days are over. The new logs stay complete so you can see exactly what happened.
  • Stronger Setup: The gateway install command is now much more stable, which means fewer headaches when you are getting started.
  • Faster Scanning: If you use WhatsApp or Matrix, you will notice that scanning QR codes is now much quicker.

How to Get the Update

Ready to try it out? It is super easy to get the latest version. Just open your terminal and type this command:

OpenClaw update

That’s it! You will be up and running with all the newest features in seconds. I can’t wait to see what you build with these new tools.
