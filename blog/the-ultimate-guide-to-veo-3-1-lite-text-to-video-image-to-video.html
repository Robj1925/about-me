Google’s New Veo 3.1 Lite: Making AI Video Easier and Cheaper

Google just released Veo 3.1 Lite, a powerful new tool that makes creating AI videos more affordable than ever. Let's look at how it works.

📅 April 1, 2026 · ⏱ 3 min read

Meet Your New Favorite AI Video Tool

Hi everyone! I’m Robby. As a software engineer, I spend a lot of time testing new AI tools to see what they can actually do. Today, I’m excited to talk about Google’s brand new model: Veo 3.1 Lite.

If you have ever wanted to turn your ideas into videos without spending a fortune, this is for you. Google designed this model to be the most affordable way to create high-quality videos using AI.

What is Veo 3.1 Lite?

Think of Veo 3.1 Lite as a super-fast, budget-friendly version of Google's bigger AI models. It’s perfect for people who need to make a lot of videos quickly—like for social media, presentations, or small projects.

Even though it is the "Lite" version, it is still very impressive. You can create videos using just text prompts or by uploading an image to bring it to life.

Why Developers and Creators Will Love It

There are a few reasons why I’m excited about this release:

  • It’s Easy on Your Wallet: It is very cost-effective, which means you can run more tests without worrying about the bill.
  • Flexible Settings: You have control over how your video looks. You can choose different lengths (4, 6, or 8 seconds), different resolutions (up to 1080p), and different screen shapes (like horizontal for computers or vertical for phones).
  • Fast Workflow: Because it is designed to be lightweight, you spend less time waiting for your video to finish and more time creating.

Big News: Prices are Dropping Soon

If you think that’s cool, wait until April 7, 2026. Google is dropping the price for the "Veo 3.1 Fast" model as well. This is great news for anyone building apps or creating content because it makes powerful AI even more accessible.

How to Get Started

You don’t need a complicated setup to try this out. You can jump right in using Google AI Studio. It is a great place to test your prompts, adjust your settings, and see the results instantly.

My Pro Tips for Success:

  1. Start Simple: Don't try to describe a whole movie in one sentence. Start with a clear, short description of what you want to see.
  2. Iterate: If the first video isn't perfect, don't worry! Change one word in your prompt and try again. That’s how we get the best results.
  3. Use Images: If you have an image you love, use the "Image-to-Video" feature. It’s a game-changer for adding movement to a still photo.

What Will You Create?

I’ve been having a blast testing this, and I’m curious to see what you come up with. Whether you are a developer looking to build a cool new app or a creator trying to spice up your videos, Veo 3.1 Lite is a tool you should definitely have in your belt.

Happy building!
