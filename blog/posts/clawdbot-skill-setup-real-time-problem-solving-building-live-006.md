## Behind the Scenes of Building AI

Hi there! I’m Robby. I’m a software engineer, and I love building cool things with AI. Most of the time, when you watch videos about coding, you only see the finished, perfect version. But that’s not how real life works!

I started my *Building Live* series to show you what it is really like to create AI systems. In my latest session, Episode 006, I sat down to add a brand-new skill to my project, Clawdbot.

## What Happens When You Build AI?

Building AI isn't just about writing code; it’s about solving puzzles. In this video, I didn't edit anything out. You get to see the whole journey, including the parts where things go wrong.

Here is what I worked on:

*   **Setting up a new skill:** I walked through the steps to connect a new feature to Clawdbot.
*   **Live debugging:** When a tool didn't work the first time, I showed exactly how I found the problem and fixed it.
*   **Integrating tools:** I talked about how to make different parts of an AI system talk to each other.
*   **Real-time problem solving:** You’ll see how I think through issues as they happen.

## Why Real-Time Coding Matters

When you are learning to code, it is easy to feel frustrated when your program hits a snag. I want you to know that even for me—someone with a Master’s degree in Computer Science—bugs happen all the time! 

By watching me build Clawdbot live, you can see how I stay calm, look at the error messages, and figure out the next step. It is all about experimenting and learning as you go.
