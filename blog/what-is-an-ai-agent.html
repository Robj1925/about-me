What Is an AI Agent? The Big Difference Between Talking and Doing

Ever wonder how AI goes from just answering questions to actually doing work for you? Discover the world of AI Agents and how they are changing the game.

📅 December 23, 2025 · ⏱ 2 min read

Hi there! I’m Robby.

I spend my days building real-world AI systems. Lately, everyone has been asking me one question: "Robby, what exactly is an AI Agent?"

If you have used ChatGPT, you know how fun it is to ask questions and get answers. But did you know there is a new, cooler version of AI that can do much more? Let’s talk about it!

Chatbots vs. AI Agents

Think of a standard chatbot like a helpful librarian. If you ask a question, the librarian gives you a great answer. But, the librarian stays behind the desk.

An AI Agent is different. An AI Agent is like a personal assistant who doesn't just answer your questions—they go out and get the job done for you.

  • Chatbots: They talk, write lists, and explain things.
  • AI Agents: They take action to finish a goal.

How Do They Actually Work?

Instead of you giving the computer a long list of steps to follow, you just give the AI Agent a goal.

For example, instead of you searching for flights, checking your calendar, and typing an email, you just tell the AI Agent: "Plan a trip for me next weekend."

The AI Agent then figures out the steps on its own:

  1. It browses the web to find flights.
  2. It checks your calendar to see when you are free.
  3. It helps book the trip.
  4. It sends an email to let you know it's done.

The Big Shift

We are moving from an era where AI just "chats" to an era where AI "acts." This means computers are becoming much more like partners that help us save time and get more done.

As someone who builds these systems, I can tell you: this is just the beginning. The future of AI isn't just about what it knows—it's about what it can do for you.
